Automated Thermo-Sanitiser

An amalgamation of Automated Sanitiser dispenser and a temperature sensor.

FEATURES

Attributes

This device is connected to automated doors in malls, shops, or any publicly accessible building. The device has a protruding part which is almost the length of an average human palm (Average male palm length: 7.6 inches and Average female palm length: 6.8 inches).

Thermal Detection

The front part of this device will have the temperature sensor which will focus the infrared energy on the wrist of the hand. Once the temperature is recorded, if it is in range with the recommended values then it will send signals to the automated door connected to the device and will open it. Otherwise, it will send alert signals and display your temperature on the screen attached to the device.

Sanitizing Technology

The latter part of the device will have an automated sanitizer dispenser. When hands are placed in the proximity of the sensor, the infrared energy quickly fluctuates. This fluctuation triggers the pump to activate and dispense the designated amount of sanitizer.
